## Common Water Leak Scenarios in Oceanside Homes

**Under-sink leaks:** Compression fittings and supply lines fail, especially in homes built before 1990. Quick repair prevents cabinet rot and mold.

**Pinhole leaks in copper pipes:** Corrosion from Oceanside's mineral-rich water creates tiny holes that spray water inside walls. Detection requires moisture mapping and sometimes thermal imaging.

**Foundation seepage:** Coastal groundwater pressure forces water through cracks in concrete. This requires both repair and waterproofing.

**Slab leaks:** Leaks under concrete foundations are common in Oceanside's older neighborhoods. They're detected with acoustic sensors and moisture mapping.

**Roof leaks from salt spray:** Oceanside's salt air accelerates roof deterioration. Leaks often appear in attics before showing on ceilings.

Each scenario demands different expertise. A company that handles all these types—not just surface leaks—is more valuable.
